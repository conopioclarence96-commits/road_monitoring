How It Works

Streamlined workflow from issue reporting to project completion

1. Report Issue

LGU officers submit road issues with photos and location data

2. Verification

Reports are verified for authenticity and accuracy

3. CIMM Integration

Approved reports sent to maintenance management system

4. Completion

Repairs are completed and validated

5. Public View

Completed projects published for public transparency
