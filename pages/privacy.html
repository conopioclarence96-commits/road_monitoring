Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2025

1. Introduction

The Road Infrastructure & Transportation Monitoring System is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our monitoring system, report road issues, or access our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Account credentials for registered users
  • Location data when reporting road issues

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Road Report Data

  • Photos and videos of road conditions
  • GPS coordinates of reported issues
  • Descriptions of infrastructure problems
  • Timestamp of reports

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Process and respond to road condition reports
  • Provide updates on infrastructure projects
  • Send traffic alerts and notifications
  • Maintain system performance and security

Communication

  • Respond to citizen inquiries and reports
  • Send system updates and maintenance notices
  • Provide requested information about road conditions

Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze traffic patterns and road usage
  • Improve system functionality and user experience
  • Identify areas requiring infrastructure attention

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • With LGU departments responsible for road maintenance and infrastructure
  • With contractors and service providers involved in road projects
  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect public safety or prevent imminent harm
  • With law enforcement agencies for official investigations

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Secure data storage and backup procedures
  • Staff training on privacy and security protocols

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your personal information (where legally permitted)
  • Restriction of processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing of your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our system uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our monitoring system.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, unless required by law to retain it longer. Road report data may be retained for historical analysis and infrastructure planning purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our system is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our system constitutes acceptance of any changes.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need to exercise your rights, please contact us:

Email: privacy@lgu.gov.ph

Phone: (555) 123-4567

Address: City Hall, Main Street, Our City

This Privacy Policy is part of the LGU Road Infrastructure & Transportation Monitoring System's commitment to transparency and citizen privacy.
