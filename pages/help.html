Frequently Asked Questions

Reporting Issues

To report a road issue:
1. Log in to your account or continue as guest
2. Click "Report Issue" on the main dashboard
3. Select the type of issue (pothole, traffic light, etc.)
4. Provide location details and upload photos if possible
5. Add description and submit
You'll receive a confirmation with tracking number.
You can report various infrastructure issues including:
• Potholes and road damage
• Traffic light malfunctions
• Street lighting problems
• Signage issues
• Drainage problems
• Bridge and sidewalk concerns
• Illegal dumping or obstructions
No, you can report issues as a guest. However, creating an account provides benefits like tracking your reports, receiving updates, and accessing historical data. Account holders also get priority notifications for issues in their areas.

Tracking Reports

After submitting a report, you'll receive a tracking number. You can:
• Log in and view your dashboard
• Enter the tracking number on the home page
• Check your email for status updates
• Enable push notifications in your account settings
• Red: High priority - Immediate attention required
• Yellow: Medium priority - Scheduled for review
• Green: Low priority - Routine maintenance
• Blue: Information - General updates or completed
• Gray: Under investigation or pending assessment

Step-by-Step Guides

First Time Reporting

  1. 1 Visit the homepage and click "Report Issue"
  2. 2 Choose issue category from dropdown menu
  3. 3 Enter location or use GPS for automatic detection
  4. 4 Upload clear photos showing the issue
  5. 5 Add detailed description and submit

Account Setup

  1. 1 Click "Login" then "Create Account"
  2. 2 Fill in personal information and verify email
  3. 3 Set your home location for personalized alerts
  4. 4 Configure notification preferences
  5. 5 Complete profile and start reporting
